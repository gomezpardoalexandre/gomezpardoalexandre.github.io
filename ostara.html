Ostara

video game, 3D

My part : Conception, UX/UI design, 3D modelling, Texturing

A story about climate change

Follow the story of a little group of Sulimo during their journey towards finding a new home.

After the drought has destroyed their home, a team of Sulimo embark on a journey to find the legendary land of Ostara. A fertil place where spring blossom all year-long. But during their climatic exode, they will need your help to overcome a lot of obstacle.

A multi-devices and multi-player game

In order to succeed, you will need to cooperate and help each other. Solve in-game puzzle and do real-life action to keep going forward. Your phone turns into a controller : move around, collect object or use your special capacity.

Meet the smuggler

One of the first obstacle is to go through the desert. No easy way around the vast arid area : the only option is to find someone who is used to travel those lands. But not everyone as your best interest in mind.
