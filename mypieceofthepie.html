My piece of the pie

web, 3D

A puzzle game with a message

My Piece of the pie is a puzzle game where you need to cut an imaginary land between four colonizating nation. But be careful ! Each King want a specific number of ressources and some of the local populations don't get along. Will you successfully cut the pie without tearing apart the continent...

This game was created during a gamjam of four days.

An impossible game

The thematic of the gamejam was to vehiculate a message through a game.

The outcome of my piece of the pie is never good. It's almost impossible to cut the continent without causing a riot or a war.And you often put aside the population in order to focus on the more manageable demand : the kings needs. A little too close to history...
