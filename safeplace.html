Safe Place

web, 3D

Breath in, breath out

It's always good to take a break, letting go for a moment of all your issues to better go on with your day. Especially, during the pandemic of Covid-19.

Safe Place offers you a unique experience of relaxation inside a digital space free of your worries.

The voice-over was writtien and recorded with a professional meditation teacher to bring you the best experience possible.

Enjoy natural sounds and disfrut of the environnement along your way. Take your time.

Embark on a relaxing journey

Explore the top of the mountain to get fresh air or go in the deep of the ocean to wash out the stress of the day. Safe Place offers many relaxing journeys to discover. Each one of them will make you more aware of yourself and embellish your safe place to make it wonderful.

Let the voice-over guide you through easy exercices to feel more relaxed. Once learned, it is easy to put them in practice every day.
