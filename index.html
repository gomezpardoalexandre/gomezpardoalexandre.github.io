About me

I'm a 23 years old graphic designer based in Paris, France. Specialized in artistic direction and interactive design, I'm passionate about creating meaningful interfaces, that make the user's experience seamless.

Throughout my journey in the wonderful world of graphic design, I've learned to associate my interest for design, human psychology and digital techniques in order to manufacture immersive experience. My curiosity is as vast as the digital world, I'm always fascinated by new technology and trying to explore the ins and outs of graphic design on every plateform. From video games to website, without forgetting motion design, I enjoy the freedom of creating polished expericence centered on the end user.

Inspired by interior design, fashion design and having a soft spot for science matters, I find myself the lucky owner of two prickly cactus, Billy and Bob, and an overflowing wardrobe.
