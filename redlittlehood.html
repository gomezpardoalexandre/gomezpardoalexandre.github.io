Little Red riding hood

app, storytelling, 3D

An auditive tale

Let your kids discover the fairy tale of Little Red riding hood in an interactive story with sonore choice. You may not know it, but the story has two very different versions. Let your choices guides you through the story according to The Grimm brothers or Perrault.

To make the right choices and get to the happy ending, you'll need to listen carefully to the different predicting noise.

An experience tailored for kids

In order to provide the best experience for kids, the UX of the application was thought with the primary user need in mind.

In order to help the kid navigate seamlessly in the experience we implemented few inputs. They are easy to reach and understandble at first glance. The experience also provide feature tailored for a young audience such as replaying sound.
